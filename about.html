Our Story

Hello, and welcome to Coffee Bliss! My name is Anshita Agrawal, and I am the founder and passionate coffee enthusiast behind this journey. We have grown from a small coffee shop to a community hub where friends, families, and coffee enthusiasts gather to enjoy our carefully crafted brews. With every cup, we strive to create moments of connection, warmth, and satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality

We believe that great coffee starts with great beans. That's why we source only the finest, ethically grown coffee from around the world.

Community

Our coffee shop is more than just a place to grab a cup. It's a space where people come together, relax, and share their stories.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging and minimizing waste wherever possible to help protect the environment.

Innovation

From traditional brews to new coffee trends, we are always exploring ways to innovate and enhance the coffee experience for our customers.
